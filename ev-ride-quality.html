Understanding EV Ride Quality.

EVs run quieter and smoother than ICE vehicles — this ePG turns that into a sales story. Advisors learn to position the 50% vs. 15% cabin-noise difference as comfort and luxury a customer can feel, not a stat to recite.

Frame the benefit

Work the scenario

A buyer likes a smooth, quiet ride but is EV-hesitant. Naturally weave in the 50% vs. 15% cabin-noise gap and lead with comfort over specs — then switch roles.
