The talking points to land
Reassurance beats specs. Pair everyday home charging with fast charging for the big trips and the concern usually dissolves.
1

Home covers daily driving

Overnight charging at home handles most daily needs — public charging is rarely needed close to home.

2

Level 3 is for the road

DC fast chargers primarily serve long-distance and road-trip travel, not the everyday commute.

3

Help find compatible stations

A key job is guiding customers to compatible chargers — bZ & bZ4X DC fast charge at both CCS1 and NACS.

4

Know the exceptions

Set expectations clearly: most PHEVs do not support Level 3 charging.
