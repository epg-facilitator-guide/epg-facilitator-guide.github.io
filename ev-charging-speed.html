The talking points to land
Four factors explain almost every "why is it charging slow?" question. Help customers set realistic expectations before they buy.
1

The car sets the ceiling

An 11 kW vehicle on a 22 kW charger still charges at 11 kW — the vehicle's max capability is the limit, not the plug.

2

Bigger battery, longer fill

Higher capacity means more to fill, so larger packs simply take longer to reach full.

3

It slows after ~80%

The vehicle eases charging past roughly 80% to protect battery health — by design, not a fault.

4

Cold weather counts

Low temperatures can slow charging and reduce efficiency until the battery warms up.
